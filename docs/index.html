VerifyFill

VerifyFill lets you connect your Google account to read verification codes and confirmation links from Gmail and autofill them on websites when you sign in.

How VerifyFill uses Google data

VerifyFill uses Google Sign-In so you can connect your Gmail account. After you sign in, the extension uses these permissions:

  • gmail.readonly — reads recent inbox messages to find verification codes and email confirmation links
  • userinfo.email — reads your Google account email address so the extension can show which account is connected

This data is used only to detect codes and links and offer to fill them on the page you are on. Email content is processed in your browser on your device. VerifyFill does not send your email to a server, does not change your inbox, and does not use your data for advertising. You can sign out in the extension or revoke access in your Google Account.
